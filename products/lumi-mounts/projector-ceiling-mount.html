Projector Ceiling Mount

PRB-G
Universal Wall & Ceiling Projector Bracket

Ideal for classrooms, offices, and home theaters, this sturdy mount supports most projectors up to 20kg (44lbs). The adjustable angle accommodates both wall and ceiling installations, catering to various projection needs including short-throw setups. Equipped with four adjustable support arms, it fits a wide range of projector mounting holes. The quick-release connector allows for swift projector removal for maintenance without the need for realignment. Its durable steel construction ensures a stable and secure installation, enhancing both functionality and convenience in any environment.

PRB-01M
Heavy-Duty Projector Ceiling Mount (For Pitched or Flat Ceiling)

Perfect for classrooms, boardrooms, and hospitality spaces, this heavy-duty mount supports projectors up to 35kg (77lbs). Designed for pitched or flat ceilings, it provides flexible positioning with pivot and tilt adjustments for optimal viewing. Made from durable heavy-gauge cold-rolled steel, it ensures stability and long-term support. The quick-release mechanism allows easy removal for maintenance without realignment. Its low-profile design keeps the projector securely mounted while maintaining a clean, unobtrusive appearance in any professional or personal setting.

TS-9
MULTI-PURPOSE TRIPOD STAND

Perfect for offices, classrooms, and professional settings, this multi-purpose tripod stand offers a sturdy and flexible solution for mounting laptops, projectors, and monitors. Featuring a 90° tiltable VESA plate, it provides optimal positioning for presentations, meetings, or workstations. The adjustable height range ensures comfortable viewing angles while supporting devices up to 15kg (33lbs). Constructed from high-quality mild steel with a powder-coated finish, it guarantees durability and long-term use. Its portable design allows for easy relocation, ensuring convenience and versatility wherever needed.
