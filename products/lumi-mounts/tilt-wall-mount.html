Tilt Wall Mount

KL22-2T
Slim Profile Tilt TV Wall Mount

Ideal for mounting 23' –42' flat-panel TVs in homes, offices, or conference rooms, this slim tilt wall mount offers both style and functionality. Designed to hold up to 35kg (77lbs), it allows you to tilt the screen for better viewing angles and make lateral adjustments for perfect positioning. The ultra-slim profile keeps the TV just 20.5mm from the wall, maintaining a sleek and modern look. Its open structure enhances airflow to components and offers easy cable access, ensuring a clean and efficient installation.

KL22-4T
Slim Profile Tilt TV Wall Mount

Perfect for mounting 32'–55' flat-panel TVs in living rooms or offices, this tilt wall mount supports up to 35kg (77lbs). Its ultra-slim profile extends only 20.5mm from the wall, blending seamlessly with modern décor. The adjustable tilt and lateral shift features help achieve the ideal viewing position. Designed for easy installation, its open structure also supports proper airflow and cable access, making it a convenient and reliable mounting solution.

KL32-4T
Super Economy Low-Profile Tilt TV Wall Mount

Suitable for TVs between 37'–80', this low-profile tilt wall mount offers strong support up to 45kg (99lbs). With a slim 20mm gap from the wall, it maintains a clean, minimal look. The basic design allows fast installation and includes ventilation-friendly open architecture. Its tilt function improves viewing comfort while the spring lock adds extra stability. This mount is a budget-friendly choice for homes or commercial spaces.

LP42-6T
X-Large Heavy-Duty Tilt TV Wall Mount

Built for large-scale installations, this heavy-duty tilt wall mount is ideal for 60'–100' TVs. With a load capacity of 75kg (165lbs), it supports heavier screens while allowing up to +5° and -10° tilt for a better viewing angle. The 85mm low-profile design ensures a sleek fit against the wall. Quick-release cords and spring-lock system provide secure and easy installation, while its wide VESA compatibility makes it versatile for home theaters or business settings.
