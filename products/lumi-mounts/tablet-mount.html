Universal Anti-Theft Tablet Countertop Kiosk

Perfect for retail counters, reception desks, and public kiosks, this universal anti-theft tablet countertop stand provides secure and flexible display options for tablets sized 7.9” to 11”. The adjustable claws support both horizontal and vertical orientations, with tilt functionality for optimal viewing angles. Silicone and EVA pads protect devices from scratches, while the lockable mechanism prevents theft or removal. Integrated cable management keeps the setup neat and professional. This stand is ideal for customer interactions, registrations, or digital displays in busy environments.

Aluminum Anti-theft Countertop Tablet Kiosk Stand

Ideal for commercial spaces, trade shows, and customer service points, this aluminum anti-theft countertop tablet kiosk stand offers secure public tablet display. Compatible with iPad mini 1/2/3/4/air, Galaxy, and most 7.9"–10.5" tablets, it features a security laptop lock and adjustable holder with a retractable leg for flexibility. The holder allows 360° rotation and ±70° tilt for optimal viewing angles. Cables can be routed through the tube for a tidy appearance, while the robust steel base ensures stability. This stand is perfect for interactive kiosks, information displays, and promotional setups in busy environments.
